‚ö†Ô∏è macOS Security Note

On first launch, macOS may show a warning that the app "is damaged and can't be opened" or cannot be verified. This is normal for apps that are not notarized with an Apple Developer certificate.

To open the app, use one of these methods:

  • Method 1: Right-click (or Ctrl+click) the app and select "Open", then click "Open" in the dialog
  • Method 2: Open Terminal and run: xattr -cr "/Applications/Docker Developer.app"

macOS will remember your choice and allow the app to open normally in the future. This is a one-time step.
